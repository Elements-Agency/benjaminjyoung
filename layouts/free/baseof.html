About Ben Young

Ben's artwork is deeply rooted in personal emotion, capturing moments of vulnerability, love, and loss. Each painting tells a heartfelt story, inviting viewers to connect with the raw feelings and experiences that inspired it. Through expressive brushwork and evocative themes, Ben creates pieces that resonate on a profound emotional level, offering comfort and reflection to those who encounter his art.

Working mainly in watercolor and sometimes oil, Ben's art has been featured in galleries and exhibitions around the US.
