Meet the Artist, Ben Young

I am a contemporary painter based in Ohio, deeply rooted in the landscapes and narratives of the Appalachian plateau where I grew up. Having transitioned from a career in software engineering, I pour lived experiences—themes of profound loss, resilience, and quiet healing—directly onto the paper.
