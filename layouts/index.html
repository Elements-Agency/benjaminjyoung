Ben mainly works with oil paint and watercolors. Occasionally drawing with watercolor pencils or oil pastels. Inspired by impressionism, he mainly paints in realism. The use of landscapes is inspired from everywhere he has traveled, from Ohio to Germany. His narratives are an expression of his own life experiences and emotions. From trauma and loss to finding peace. The brushwork, an abstract expression in to every detail.
