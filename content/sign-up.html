The Story Behind the Paintings

{{< preview painting="/artwork/one-last-walk-with-izzy/" caption="One Last Walk With Izzy" style="right small" >}}

Every painting I create is more than a composition of color and form — it’s an emotional narrative drawn from lived experience, memory, and the moments that shape a life. My work blends intimate human feeling with narrative realism, inviting you not just to see the art, but to feel it.

From quiet moments of reflection and loss to the resilience found in healing, each piece serves as a chapter in a larger story. Whether it’s the gentle quiet of Coming Home, a testament of grief and hope in Final Embrace, or the tender warmth amid shadow in In The Dark, my paintings sit at the intersection of emotional truth and visual expression.

Meet Ben Young

{{< img span="image right" class="rounded" src="/images/ben-small.webp" alt="Photo of Benjamin J. Young" title="Ben Young" >}}

Ben Young is a watercolorist and storyteller whose work blends emotion, memory, and imagination. His paintings capture intimate human experiences—moments of love, loss, and reflection—alongside the quiet beauty of nature. Each piece invites viewers into a narrative, offering not just an image, but a feeling to take home.

By joining Ben’s newsletter, you’ll step inside his creative world—receiving first looks at new works, insights from his studio, and opportunities to collect his art with exclusive subscriber benefits.
