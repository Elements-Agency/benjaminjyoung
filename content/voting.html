Patina Arts Centre First Fundraiser

Join us for an unforgettable evening as we celebrate our vibrant arts community at Patina Arts Centre's Inaugural Fundraiser. As a cornerstone of artistic expression and innovation, Patina Arts Centre is dedicated to fostering a dynamic environment where art thrives and creativity flourishes.

Date: Friday, August 2, 2024
Time: 5:00pm - 10:00pm
Location: Patina Arts Centre, 324 Cleveland Ave NW Canton, Ohio 44702
Admission: Free to attend!

Go To Facebook Event

Event Highlights:
- Art Exhibition: Experience a curated exhibition showcasing works celebrating a diverse array of styles and mediums.
- Live Performances: Enjoy captivating performances from local comedians at our Free Comedy show hosted by Canton Comedy Boom.
- Silent Auction: Bid on exclusive artworks, unique experiences, and special items generously donated by artists and supporters. All proceeds will directly benefit the continued growth and sustainability of Patina Arts Centre.

Why Attend?

Your presence at this event will support Patina Arts Centre's mission to provide a welcoming space for artistic expression, cultural engagement, and educational opportunities. Funds raised from this gala will enable us to expand our programming, offer more classes and workshops, and continue to host free public events that enrich our community.

Tickets:
Admission to the event is free, but we encourage you to participate in our silent auction and consider making a donation to support our efforts.

Join us on this special First Friday for a night of inspiration, connection, and celebration. Together, we can ensure that Patina Arts Centre remains a center for creativity and culture in our community. We look forward to welcoming you to this special event!
